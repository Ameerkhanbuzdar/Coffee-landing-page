Our Story

Coffee, Craft &
Community

Bloom Coffee began in 2013 with a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee could change the pace of your day. We source directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Indonesia — paying above fair-trade prices because we believe great coffee starts with great relationships.

Every bean is roasted in our micro-roastery next door in small batches, ensuring you always get coffee at peak flavor. Our baristas are certified Q Graders who treat every drink as a craft project.

Direct Trade
We know our farmers by name and visit annually.
In-House Roasting
Roasted fresh every Tuesday and Friday.
Sustainability
